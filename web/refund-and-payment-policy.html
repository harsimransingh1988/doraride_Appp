Refund & Payment Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

This Refund & Payment Policy explains how payments, refunds, cancellations, and wallet transactions work on DoraRide (“DoraRide”, “we”, “our”, “us”).

It applies to all users (riders and drivers) using our mobile apps (Android/iOS) and website.

1. Payments

DoraRide allows riders to make payments for trips using:

  • Credit / Debit Cards
  • Digital payment methods supported by our payment partners (e.g., Stripe)
  • DoraRide Wallet (if available)

All payments are processed securely by third-party payment providers. DoraRide does not store full card details on its servers.

Prices shown in the app may include:

  • Trip fare
  • Platform/service fees
  • Optional extras (premium seats, extra luggage, etc.)

Currency is displayed before confirmation and charged accordingly.

2. Wallet Payments

  • DoraRide Wallet balance can be used to pay for eligible trips.
  • Wallet top-ups are non-refundable once credited, except where required by law or due to a technical error.
  • Wallet balance cannot be transferred to other users unless explicitly allowed.
  • Withdrawals (if enabled) may be subject to verification and processing time.

3. Rider Cancellations & Refunds

Refund eligibility depends on trip status, timing, and cancellation reason:

A. Rider Cancels Before Trip Starts

  • If a rider cancels within the allowed cancellation window, a full or partial refund may be issued.
  • Platform fees may be non-refundable.

B. Late Cancellations

Cancellations close to departure time may result in:

  • Partial refund
  • No refund

This helps protect drivers from losses.

C. No-Shows

If a rider does not arrive and does not cancel:

  • No refund is provided
  • Repeated no-shows may lead to account restrictions

4. Driver Cancellations

  • If a driver cancels a confirmed trip, riders may receive a full refund, including platform fees (where applicable).
  • Repeated driver cancellations may result in penalties or account action (see Driver Cancellation Policy).

5. Trip Issues & Disputes

Refunds may be considered if:

  • The trip was not completed due to driver fault
  • The trip was materially different from what was confirmed
  • A verified safety or service issue occurred

Each case is reviewed individually. Refunds are not guaranteed and depend on investigation results.

6. Processing Time

Approved refunds are usually processed within 5–10 business days. Actual credit time depends on:

  • Payment method
  • Bank or card issuer

DoraRide is not responsible for delays caused by banks or payment providers.

7. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds are not issued for:

  • Completed trips without issues
  • Rider no-shows
  • Policy violations
  • Wallet top-ups already used
  • Fraudulent activity or abuse of the platform

8. Fraud & Abuse

DoraRide reserves the right to:

  • Deny refunds in cases of fraud or misuse
  • Suspend or terminate accounts involved in refund abuse
  • Recover losses where legally permitted

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund & Payment Policy at any time. Changes take effect once published. Continued use of DoraRide means acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or want to request a refund review:

Email: support@doraride.com
Website: https://doraride.com
