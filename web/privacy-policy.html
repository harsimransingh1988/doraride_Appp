Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2025

DoraRide ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "Application") and related services (collectively, the "Services"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE WITH THE TERMS OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY, PLEASE DO NOT ACCESS THE APPLICATION.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register with the Application, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and Services, when you participate in activities on the Application, or otherwise contact us. The personal information we collect may include:

  • Contact Data: Name, email address, phone number
  • Account Credentials: Passwords, security information for authentication
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, display name, preferences
  • Payment Information: Payment card details (processed securely by our payment processors)
  • Government ID: For drivers, we may collect driver's license information and other verification documents

1.2 Location Information

To provide our ride-hailing services, we collect precise or approximate location information from your mobile device when the app is running in the foreground or background, including:

  • Pick-up and Drop-off Locations
  • Real-time Location During Rides (for drivers and riders)
  • Frequent Locations (such as home and work addresses)

1.3 Usage and Device Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our Services, including:

  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, search queries
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies

1.4 Communication Data

We collect information when you contact us for support or communicate with other users through the Application, including:

  • Customer Support Messages
  • Ratings and Reviews
  • Chat Messages between drivers and riders

1.5 Transaction Information

We collect information related to your use of our Services, including:

  • Ride Details: Date, time, duration, distance, fare
  • Payment History: Transaction amounts, payment methods
  • Promotions and Referrals

2.3 Safety and Security

  • To ensure the safety of our users and prevent fraudulent activity
  • To comply with legal obligations and law enforcement requests
  • To investigate and address conduct that may violate our Terms of Service
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

2.4 Communications

  • To send administrative information, such as updates to our terms and policies
  • To send promotional communications (with your consent where required)
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

2.5 Legal Compliance

  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • To protect the rights, property, and safety of our users and the public
